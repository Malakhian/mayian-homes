Cancellation Policy

Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both our guests and our business operations.

Standard Cancellation

  • Short stays (1-6 nights): Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in
  • Long stays (7-14 nights): Free cancellation up to 7 days before check-in
  • Extended stays (15+ nights): Free cancellation up to 14 days before check-in

Commitment Fees

  • Cancellations within 48 hours of check-in: 100% of first night's stay
  • No-shows: 100% of entire reservation cost
  • Early departures: 50% of remaining nights

Special Circumstances

We understand emergencies happen. For documented medical emergencies or travel restrictions, we may waive fees at management's discretion.

House Rules

To ensure a pleasant stay for all guests, we require adherence to these policies:

Occupancy & Identification

  • Maximum 6 guests on the property at any time
  • All guests must provide government-issued ID at booking
  • We require nationality and purpose of stay for regulatory compliance
  • Passport/ID numbers must be verified upon arrival

Property Policies

  • Strictly no parties or events
  • Quiet hours: 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM
  • No smoking in rooms (designated outdoor areas only)
  • No pets on premise

Security Deposit

A Ksh. 5,000 security deposit is required at check-in, refundable after inspection of the room.
